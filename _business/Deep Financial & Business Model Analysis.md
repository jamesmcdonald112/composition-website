## 🧠 **PRICING PSYCHOLOGY FOR SOLICITORS**

### **Risk Perception**
Fixed fees are often maligned by solicitors and relegated to commoditised work, such as will drafting or residential conveyancing. This is a massive oversight, as fixed fees are used widely nowadays. 

**Key Insight:** Solicitors understand and value predictable pricing - they use it themselves.

### **Professional Service Buying Behavior**
Annual subscriptions reverse the decision process. Instead of minimizing commitment, they require customers to make it upfront. Customers who pay annually are less likely to churn.

**Implication:** Offer annual discounts to lock in longer commitments and reduce churn.

### **Value Perception Anchoring**
The fixed fee should include a premium, for removing the risk of uncertainty for the client. Any client who is risk averse will gladly pay for this benefit.

**Application:** Position your subscription as risk removal - no surprise website costs, guaranteed compliance.

**Financial Reality:**
