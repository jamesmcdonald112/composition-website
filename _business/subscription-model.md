# Website Subscription Model — Business Idea

## The Core Idea

Build premium solicitor websites and charge a monthly subscription instead of a large upfront fee. You retain ownership of the code. The client pays monthly for as long as the site is live.

This is already how some Irish companies operate (Solicitor Site etc.) but none of them are building at a premium design level. That's the gap.

---

## Why Solicitors Are the Right Niche

- They understand retainer-based billing — it's how they charge their own clients
- Conservative with large capital spend but comfortable with monthly professional fees
- There are ~2,000+ solicitor practices in Ireland, most with mediocre websites
- The existing "specialist" Irish solicitor web agencies are doing basic WordPress template work
- No one in Ireland is building solicitor sites at a premium Astro/GSAP level — essentially zero direct competition at the top end

---

## Pricing Structure

Offer two options upfront — let the client choose:

### Option A — Subscription (recommended)
- Upfront cost: €0
- Monthly fee: €200 – €250
- Who owns the code: You
- Minimum term: 12 months, then rolling monthly
- If they cancel: Site comes down, their content returned to them

### Option B — Buy-Out
- Upfront cost: €8,000 – €15,000
- Monthly fee: €80 – €100 (hosting + maintenance only)
- Who owns the code: Them
- If they cancel: They keep everything

Most clients will choose Option A because the upfront number is off-putting. But giving the choice means nobody feels trapped or misled — especially important when your client is a solicitor who will read the contract.

### Buy-Out During Subscription
Offer clients the ability to buy out at any point:
- After 12 months: €6,000
- After 24 months: €4,000
- After 36 months: €2,500

This rewards loyalty, keeps clients happy, and is a good sales tool ("the longer you stay, the cheaper ownership gets").

---

## What the Monthly Fee Covers

At €200 – €250/month the client gets:

- Hosting (Netlify paid plan — your cost ~€15–€19/month)
- Uptime monitoring (UptimeRobot — free)
- SSL certificate (automatic on Netlify — zero effort)
- Security — Astro is static-first, minimal attack surface vs WordPress
- Up to 2 hours of content changes per month (phone number, staff change, updated copy)
- Monthly SEO report (Google Search Console summary — ~20 mins work)
- Annual review call

**Your real time per client per month once live: 1–2 hours maximum.**

At 10 clients: €2,000 – €2,500/month recurring for ~15–20 hours of work.

## What to Charge for Photography

You don't do photography — source a local commercial photographer and either:
- Partner with them (refer work to each other, you mark up their rate)
- Include stock images in the base subscription, offer real photography as a paid upsell

A commercial photography half-day shoot in Ireland costs €400 – €800. You can charge €800 – €1,200 and include it as a premium add-on. Clients at the top end will want real photos — stock images undermine the premium positioning of the site.

---

## The Contract — Key Points

Must be in writing and signed before work begins. Solicitor clients will read it.

Key clauses to include:
- You own the code and design
- They own their content (their text, their logo, their own photos)
- Clear statement that the site is licensed, not sold, under the subscription model
- Minimum 12-month term, then rolling monthly with 30 days notice
- What happens if they stop paying (see below)
- Buy-out option and pricing schedule

---

## If a Client Stops Paying

Do it in stages — never abrupt:

1. Payment missed → email reminder, 7 days grace period
2. Still unpaid after 7 days → site goes to a simple holding page
3. 30 days unpaid → site taken down, their content returned as a zip file (text, logos, images)

As long as this process is in the contract and fair notice is given, this is legally sound in Ireland. You are withdrawing a service they stopped paying for — same as any utility or SaaS provider.

**Do not take the site down overnight over one missed payment.** The legal position may be fine but the reputational damage with a solicitor client is not worth it.

## The Competitive Advantage

- Astro 6 + GSAP (parallax, scroll triggers, DrawSVG) — nobody in the Irish solicitor niche is building at this level
- Premium photography direction and image curation
- Full SEO setup out of the box (structured data, canonical URLs, OG tags, sitemap)
- Contact form with spam protection and email delivery built in
- Design quality at the level of Awwwards-winning studios — not a WordPress template

The existing Irish "specialist" solicitor web agencies (Solicitor Site, Web Wizard, Nua Soft) are doing €3,500 – €8,000 WordPress sites. A premium subscription product at €200 – €250/month with genuinely exceptional design has no real competition.
